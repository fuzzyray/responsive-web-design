Biography

Chief Varner began his fire service career with the Phoenix (AZ) Fire Department in 1967. His fire service experience includes administration, operations, communications, safety, airport operations, training, fire prevention and fire investigations. He has taught numerous fire service training classes throughout the United States and is recognized for his expertise in firefighter protective clothing and safety issues.

Chief Varner holds an Associate of Arts Degree in Fire Science and a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration.
